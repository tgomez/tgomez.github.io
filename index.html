Crash

Los Angeles is a large commuter city which inherently lends itself to numerous traffic collisions. Car crashes are one of the leading causes of death in the US and as such they are an ideal candidate for study. Studying a leading cause of death may unveil insights which may help prevent or reduce deaths due to traffic collisions. Technologies/Techiniques used: This is a full stack application. Data was collected, cleaned with Python Pandas and NumPy libraries and stored in an SQLite database. A website was created utilizing HTML/CSS/Bootstrap and visualizations were created with Javascript and Plotly.

Technologies used: Coordinates of random cities were generated with NumPy. The coordinates were assigned cities and the weather data was collected by making API calls to OpenWeatherMap. Data was then cleaned and visualized with MatPlotLib.
